Hey! I'm Parth — welcome to my little island of the internet. I'm studying mathematics and computer science at École Polytechnique in Paris, and I spend most of my time thinking about complex analysis, number theory, combinatorics, probability & algorithms.

I've done some budding research in these areas and have been fortunate to collaborate with Christophe Vignat and others along the way.

When I'm not doing math, you'll probably find me messing around with code on GitHub or exploring France. I'm always happy to chat — feel free to reach out!
